RP Women's Retreat 2018
The Tongue and the Christian Woman

About

Speaker:  Sharon Sampson
Topic:   The Tongue and the Christian Woman

Location: Elkhart RPC

Dates: November 2-3, 2018

How has God called Christian women to use their tongues? Come and be encouraged to consider the various ways we can honor the Lord with our speech, while considering how the very design of our tongues can help us to understand our calling as Christian women who speak the truth in love. We are really looking forward to this year's retreat, and we hope to see you there!
