Information

What to Bring: Snack or drink to share, Bible, Blue Psalter, pen, notebook, personal items, extra blanket, flashlight and your favorite game.
*Linens and Towels provided

Arrival: Follow the signs to the Welcome Center upon arrival. Check in at the registration table where you will sign-in, drop snacks off and settle in.

Meals: Meals will be served in the Dining Hall which is located in the same building as the Large Chapel. Highland Lakes provides healthy meals, providing full-service buffet-style dining. We are sorry for any inconvenience, but the camp offers no dietary accommodations and is unable to provide a menu beforehand. For special dietary needs, please plan to bring your own meals.

Fellowship: Highland Lakes offers a kitchenette, coffee bar and lounging area in the Welcome Center. A fireplace, couches and games make this the perfect place to visit.
